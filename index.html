ABOUT ME

SOUND ENGINEER & AUDIO TECHNICIAN

With over 5 of experience in the audio industry, I specialize in creating immersive soundscapes that transport listeners to other worlds. My work spans feature films, short form content and experimental audio installations.

I approach sound as a living entity, carefully crafting each element to serve the narrative and emotional core of the project. From the subtle rustle of leaves to earth-shaking explosions, every sound tells a story.
